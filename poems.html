Words I couldn’t draw.

← back to museum
You are my sun and I’m a moth.
I know we can’t stay together.
Still, I keep drifting toward your warmth,
not because I am cold,
but because your warmth is my whole world.
I want to be with you. I only want you.
I know I shouldn’t,
but I keep falling for it.
I know it won’t work,
still, your grace is enough to quiet my heartbeat.
Not as beautiful as you, my love.
You are an angel and the devil,
a bliss and a temptation at the same time.
I can’t move on from her,
not because I can’t,
it’s because I don’t want to.
She was my whole world,
and I couldn’t protect my world.
All the promises are just words left in the past.
I lost her.
And some days it feels like I lost myself too.
This isn’t poetry for perfection.
This is just me leaving pieces of myself behind.

This is not literature. This is memory.
